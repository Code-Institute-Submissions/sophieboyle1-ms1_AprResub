hey there, I'm Rosalie

Based In Dublin

After travelling extensively and living in Canada, I have now thankfully returned home to Ireland, where I now works across the realms of architecture and interiors, fashion, travel and commercial photography. Having been widely published from the Irish Arts Review to Condé Nast Brides magazine throughout my career thus far – plus exhibiting in Paris and New York and winning photography competitions in Toronto – I can additionally list brand development, having worked with brands such as that of internationally renowned jeweller Rudolf Heltzel.
